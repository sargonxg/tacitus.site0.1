01. THE PROBLEM

>> The Resolution Deficit

Your organization generates conflict at the speed of cloud storage, but you resolve it at the speed of human memory. We call this the Resolution Deficit.

Critical signals are buried in reply-all chains. Narratives fragment across departments. Leaders are forced to reconstruct timelines from scratch for every crisis. Tacitus does not replace decision-makers; it gives them a map.

Context Fragmentation

Facts are scattered across Slack, Email, and PDF attachments. Tacitus unifies them into a single, queryable timeline.

Narrative Blindness

Conflict isn't just about facts; it's about stories. We detect the hidden "identity frames" that make disputes intractable.

Cognitive Overload

Stop reading 5,000 emails to understand a dispute. Get a structured "State of Conflict" brief in minutes.
